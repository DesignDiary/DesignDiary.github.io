Product 1: Airpods 4

Introduction/ Context

The Apple Air pods are headphones that work seamlessly with all Apple products. They have noise cancellation and are easy to integrate with your everyday life.

Mapping

Mapping is related to layout of controls and how they affect the product. In regard to the air pods they connect seamlessly to all apple devices that are associated with an apple account. On the physical design of device there are touch pods at the end of the earbud that control whether the audio is paused or calls are answers.

MAYA

MAYA is the balance of what makes the product stand out and the familiarity of design. The design of the air pods is simple but a well-known stable considering that they are on their 4th variation. A reason for it standing out is great audio quality, wireless, and seems to handle your everyday phone functions naturally such as calls while also providing entertainment in the form of music/podcasts. It’s the standard earbud design but without the harsh piece of plastic that goes into your ear. It seems to be universal sizing for the air pods coming from someone that often has to change out to the smallest ear bud size.

Aesthetic-Usability Effect

The aesthetic usability effect is the idea that things that are beautiful in design are easier to use. The design of the air pods is simple but pleasing in the sense that they are not overly complicated. There are not a lot of buttons on it or many things to learn about it. For the most part it is connected to Bluetooth and you’re ready to go.

Veblen Effect

A belief that the price equates to the quality. It is a common belief among Americans that you get what you paid for. The prices are displayed below the picture at the top. Although I agree that the air pods are of excellent quality and are simple to integrate into daily life, they are rather pricey.

Sunk Cost Effect

A habit of investing money into something because one has done so in the past. This can be said about the product due to the iterations it has had. Despite not too much changing from generation-to-generation people are still more than willing to buy the latest as soon as they can.

Final Recommendation

The Air pods are great to use whether for listening to music, answering phone calls, or staying up to date on messages coming your way. The mapping on this device is seamless and easy to use. The only downfall would be the cost of the Air pods since they are on the expensive side of things. Overall, though as a new Apple user I would highly recommend the Air pods since the audio quality is amazing even if you don’t go for the option that has active noise cancelling.
