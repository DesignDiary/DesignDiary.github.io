HOME META EVALUATION

The team decided to go with a website to keep it simple and easy to follow. The home page is there to guide users through the products that we decided to review and if they click onto it then they are met with the full evaluation. Its layout is organized and makes the content easy to scan over. The product images help the user if they are looking for more details on a particular product and help keep the page visually balanced. Finally, we went with a minimal color palette so that the user is not distracted and stay focused on the content of the website.
